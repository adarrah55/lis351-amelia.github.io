My name is Amelia, and I love listening to music. I am currently a sophomore at the University of Wisconsin-Madison studying Information and Data Science. I am originally from Chicago and started going to concerts at a young age. Chicago is a great place for music lovers because of all of the amazing music venues and festivals in the area. Some of my favorite places to see concerts in Chicago include the Aragon Ballroom, the Riviera Theatre, and Schubas Tavern. On this website, I'll share some of the songs I've been enjoying lately, the artists I've been listening to, and my top genres (with some playlists!) for the past few months.
